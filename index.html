About Me

I previously worked for a large financial services company, but was laid off in October of 2018 (just shy of my 30th anniversary) after they announced that they were leaving the Denver area. Although the company has other offices in Michigan and Tennessee, my wife and I decided that we didn't want to relocate.

For a couple of reasons, the layoff was a blessing for us. Not only did I receive a generous severance package, but that severance allowed us to downsize our home (which we had talked about doing for some time), and allowed me to enroll in a Full Stack Flex Web Devlopment bootcamp at the University of Denver.
